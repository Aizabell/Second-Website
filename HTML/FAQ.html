Frequently asked questions (FAQ)

  Q.   Which regions are available for Delivery Services ?

  A.   We operate our delivery services in Yangon only. But we also do express delivery to other parts of Myanmar.



  Q.   How long does it take to arrive ?

  A.   Our delivery service may takes 2 work days within Yangon and 10-15 work days


  Q.   Is it possible to get a refund ?

  A.   We only accepts refund when you cancelled the order within 3 days from the day you ordered or the item is defective according to our policy.






  Q.   How much spending needed to get delivery free ?

  A.   If the total purchase in one order is higher than 400$ , we offer free delivery charges.


  Q.   How long does it take to restock ?

  A.   It usually takes one week to restock in normal occasion but if it is highly demanded , it will take 2 weeks to restock.


  Q.   Which Payment methods are accepted for delivery ?

  A.   We only accepts credit card payment as the cash system on delivery is not reliable.
